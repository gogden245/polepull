About Us

Let's get acquainted.

Our Story

We were snowboarding one powdery morning. To our dismay, we found ourselves on a cat track (a very flat stretch of trail- a snowboarder's worst nightmare) and started to slow down almost to a stop. We were stuck. A group of kind skiers came around the bend next and yelled as they passed by, "Need a pull?" They extended their ski poles and tugged us along until we had enough momentum. Thus, our name was born. Pole Pull. Skiers helping other skiers. Sometimes a little pull is all it takes.

Our Goal

Pole Pull is an organization aimed at making skiing and snowboarding more affordable. We want to “give you a pull.” Our most recent project will make Sundance ski passes more accessible to college students by creating a donation campaign to subsidize their prices. We hope that everyone will be able to enjoy the slopes this season. We know that with the help of our sponsors and donors, we will be able to make Pole Pull’s dream a reality.

Our Program

We've helped Sundance create a program where ski pass holders can give various donations towards a “ski scholarship” as they purchase an upgraded ski pass. A ski scholarship is a fund to help college students pay for a ski pass who otherwise might not be able to. Those who donate can receive benefits such as a fast pass, reserved parking, discounted meals, and more. Students can apply to received this scholarship and earn it based on their grades and needs. More About Our Program

Executive Summary

The Pole Pull Pass Program is a project designed to provide subsidized ski lift tickets at Sundance Resort to poor college students. This program supplies eligible students with 50% off adult night skiing season lift tickets to Sundance.

Funding for this program is generated in two ways:

1. Skiers can purchase special Pole Pull season tickets through Sundance Resort. These tickets are more expensive than standard adult season tickets purchased through Sundance; however, Poll Pull season tickets provide patrons with bonus perks not offered by regular passes. The difference in prices between these passes and base passes will go towards the fund. Pricing and perks for these tickets are illustrated below.

2. Skiers can donate directly to the fund through Pole Pull. These donations are tax deductible because Pole Pull is a non-profit 501(c)(3).

The money raised each season determines how many students will receive discounted passes.

Eligibility for this program is determined by a merit-based scholarship program run through Pole Pull. Students interested in the program will fill out an application indicating their interest. Pole Pull will review these applications and determine which students will receive the discounted passes. Discounted passes will be allocated to students based on several criteria, including financial needs and academic performance.

Selected students will receive access to a discounted night skiing season pass. Adult season passes cost $400 per season. Eligible students will purchase these subsidized tickets for $200.

Funding for the program will only go towards subsidizing these special lift tickets. Students will not receive discounts for gear rentals at the resort.

Providing subsidized passes to poor college students will boost revenue for Sundance Resort. Many of these college students may continue to spend money at Sundance Resort when they have more money.

We can only give as much as we receive so it varies each season.
